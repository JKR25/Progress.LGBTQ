2010:




Ang Ladlad, an LGBTQ political party, applies for registration with the Commission on Elections (COMELEC) but is initially denied. They later win a case in the Supreme Court, allowing them to participate in elections. (April 8, 2010)

The Philippine Psychiatric Association removes homosexuality from its list
of mental disorders.
