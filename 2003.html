2003:




Global Divas is a detailed study of the worldwide and cross-border aspects of gay identity experienced by Filipino immigrants in New York City. This ethnography challenges assumptions regarding the linear progression of a unified gay culture and the eventual integration of all LGBTQ+ individuals into a modern gay society.
